About Me

Hello! My name is Annie Kong and I am a current 4th year student studying Finance, Statistics, and Data Science at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign.

My passion is centered around consulting, product management, and business/data analytics. This past summer I had the opportunity to serve as a Financial Services Consulting Intern for Crowe and contribute to data-driven projects across multiple sectors. I enjoy bridging the gap between complex data and practical application, blending my analytical expertise with strategic insights to innovate and optimize. My goal is to develop products that not only excel in functionality but are also intuitive an duser-centric, ensuring they deliver scalabe and effective outcomes. By combining rigourous data analysis with a clear understanding of user needs, I strive to create seamless, efficient experiences that drive business success and transform how industries operate.
